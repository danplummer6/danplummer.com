About Me



Hello, my name is Dan Plummer and I create beautiful web solutions for small businesses and start-ups. I have a bachelors degree in Web Design & Development and first taught myself to code back in 2010.


I am Minnesota born and raised and currently call Edina, MN home. I attended school in Winter Park, FL at Full Sail University and moved back to Minnesota shortly after graduation. During my studies I learned how to create cross-platform websites & applications, as well as what it takes to take projects from the idea phase to development and beyond.
